I am a PhD student at Computer Science Department at School of Computer Science, Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Prof. Ding Zhao and Prof. Fei Fang. I also worked with Prof. Jeff Schneider. My research interests lie in Multimodal Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Robotics, Language Grounding, and Healthcare.

Before coming to CMU, I received my B.Eng. from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Bao-Liang Lu.
